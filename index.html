About Partners International Brokers

Brief History

With a solid background and a long experience in the Gulf Region, a group of highly qualified and dedicated experts have decided to found PIB (Partners International Brokers) and launch its operations in Lebanon.

Thanks to that exposure, a large network of contacts and a knowledge built through many years, PIB was able to gain access to a wide panel of insurance companies within the MENA region, especially in KSA, Bahrain and Lebanon.

It is greatly advantageous that PIB has a very strong and durable access to first class Insurance and Reinsurance Securities. Moreover, PIB proved to outshine in establishing stout and sound business ties with First Class Reinsurance Securities throughout the world.

Strategic Plan

The first step in our long business path was opening our head office in Beirut. PIB counts on branching out all over Lebanon to reach all potential clientele and satisfy their diversified needs.

Furthermore, PIB will be expanding regionally to Bahrain, UAE, and Oman. With regards to our KSA clients, PIB trusts they will always be closely served through our sister companies in the Kingdom.

PIB insurance and reinsurance consultancy experts will always make the best use of their distinction, dedication and professionalism in analyzing each and every client to fully understand their needs and evaluating their risk portfolio in order to provide them with a comprehensive clear study and deliver them with the most suitable course of action along with the most competitive terms and conditions from reputable insurance companies.
